"Welcome to KeeperRL Alpha 30! This patch was released on June 3rd, 2020.

A lot of changes have arrived with this update. Below is a very short summary, and we encourage you to check out the full change log at keeperrl.com.

* A new, playable Gnomes faction.
* Support for loading multiple mods at once.
* Expansion of the White Knight Keeper.
* A hidden dungeon and two new secret levels.
* The option to build your base in 5 different biomes.

If this is your first time playing KeeperRL, remember to start with the tutorial!"
